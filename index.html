I'm So Sorry, Babe

My dearest Babe,

I know that what you saw in your dream hurt you deeply, and even though it was just a dream, the pain it caused you is real, and for that, I am truly sorry. I never want to be the cause of your tears or make you feel insecure about us.

You are the only one for me, Glo. My heart beats only for you. I understand why it affected you so much, and I want you to know that I would never do anything to betray your trust or hurt our beautiful relationship.

Please forgive me, my love. Let me spend every day proving to you that you are my everything.

With all my love,

A Letter From My Heart

My Dearest Babe Glo,

As our birthday approaches, I find myself reflecting on how lucky I am to have you in my life. You are my sunshine, my happiness, my everything.

I know I made a mistake, and I carry the weight of knowing I caused you pain. But please believe me when I say that you are the only person I see, the only person I want, and the only person I love.

Every moment without your smile feels incomplete. Every day I wake up thinking of you, and every night I fall asleep dreaming of our future together.

I promise to be better, to be more understanding, and to always make you feel secure in our love. You deserve nothing less than the world, and I will spend every day trying to give it to you.

Happy early birthday, my love. May this year bring us closer than ever before.

Forever and Always Yours,

Leizer
